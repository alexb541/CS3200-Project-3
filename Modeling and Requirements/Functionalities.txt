These following redis structures use various attributes from the UML model, except from the
TeamMember entity.

Team Leaderboard:
At each competition, each team is ranked by their scores.

Team Stats Cache:
Each team at each competition has their own set of competition attributes, including
avgAuto, avgTeleop, avgEndgame, climbSuccessRate, reportCount, lastUpdated.

Recent Scouting Feed:
A list of notifications of scouting reports that have occurred at a competition.
